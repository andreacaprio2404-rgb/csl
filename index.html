Questions

Before You Decide

What exactly happens in the first 90 days?
You begin with a private Sprint Blueprint Call with Andrea to personalize your path. Then: strip to one premium offer, choose one lead source, and install the architecture — Lead Source → Entry Event → Sales Conversation. By month 3 you have a working system, not a plan.
Can I keep my current offers?
Sprint rules require one offer only. If your current offer is priced at $1,000+ and is premium-positioned, you keep it. Multiple offers get paused for 90 days — no exceptions. This is the rule that makes the Sprint work.
Is this a group programme or 1:1?
Primarily group — bi-weekly calls, async support, hot seats. All members begin with a private Sprint Blueprint Call. PIF and VIP members also have ongoing direct async access to Andrea. Group container with 1:1 precision applied inside it.
What if I don't get results in 90 days?
We promise traction, not income guarantees. Follow the Sprint framework and you will have active conversations by month 3. If you don't, Andrea rebuilds your blueprint with you in a private session at no charge. See the guarantee section above for full details.
Can I cancel after month 3?
Yes — on the monthly plan, you can cancel with 30 days' notice after month 3. Most members stay because the Lab continues building on the Sprint. No lock-in beyond the initial 3-month commitment. PIF members have full 12-month access with no ongoing charge.
What's the difference between Beta, PIF, and VIP?
Beta ($497/mo) is the monthly entry — full access, founding rate, cancel after month 3. PIF ($3,997) is one payment for 12 months — saves $8,403 vs standard, includes the Retreat 50% discount, and removes the monthly decision. VIP ($7,597) adds a monthly 15-minute private strategy wire direct to Andrea and a priority Blueprint Call. All three begin with the same Sprint.
What does the Sprint Blueprint Call involve?
It's a private onboarding session with Andrea — not a generic call. You leave with your specific offer locked, lead source chosen, and first 30 days mapped. No generic advice. No waiting to get started. You're executing within 48 hours.
I'm already above $7K/month. Is this relevant?
The Sprint is designed for $3–7K. If you're above that, the Private Structure Session is the better starting point — a 45-minute diagnostic that identifies your specific constraint before recommending any programme. Book one here.
